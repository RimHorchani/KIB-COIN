About Us

KIB COIN is a large restaurant,a restaurant-controlled online food ordering, the restaurants create their own website and app, or choose to hire a delivery vendor.They make sure to obtain software that manages the orders efficiently, meaning it has the capability to manage different orders at once. When they hire a vendor, the restaurant pays for a monthly fee or percentage-based fees. The vendor covers the developmental costs. A customer can choose to have the food for pick-up.The process consists of a customer choosing the restaurant of their choice, scanning the table's QRcode, choosing a family than an item, and finally choosing for pick-up . Payment is then administered by paying in cash at the restaurant when going to pickup.
